About Me

Bio

My avatar spawned in Santa Barbara, California. I had min-maxed my stats to be very intelligent, both mentally and emotionally, but balanced out by asthma, which was extremely physically limiting; and depression, which was emotionally and mentally limiting. Fortunately, my initial party was made up of two amazing parents and four siblings.

Before I had finished the newbie zone, my parents directed the party up to the Oregon coast to a small town called Tillamook. I finished up the newbie zone by the time I was 18, and decided to keep leveling in college. College didn't go so well, so I went back to the newbie zone to figure something else out. I did this several times. The problem was that I was trying to level up my emotional skills in relationships, but was told by everyone else to focus on my mental skills in classes.

It ended up taking me several years of on-and-off courses to graduate college, but I finally did it. I got a B.S. in philosophy, which had thoroughly taught me that reason and argument are equally as effective and valid as physically forcing someone to do something; in general both are simply matters of convincing the other person that it's just not worth arguing/fighting with you. Or, in extreme cases, removing their option to do so at all.

I continued leveling my emotional skills as much as possible. There were many adventures and failures, some worthy of becoming stories, some less so. It wasn't until I started to notice that no one else was leveling alongside me that I realized that I had very nearly reached max level. At least, I was no longer able to easily find the zones that were high enough level for me to continue to advance. It was also around that time that I discovered that emotional skills, no matter how high level, do not make a lot of money.

That brings us to now. Now, I'm grinding my mental skills in web engineering in order to reach a place of financial stability, at which point I can more easily access higher level content.
